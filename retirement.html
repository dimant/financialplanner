Plan Your Retirement

This tool helps you see how your retirement savings might grow and how long they could last. You enter your Current Savings, Annual Savings Until Retirement, Years Until Retirement, Years in Retirement, and Annual Expenses in Retirement. You also set some assumptions about the future, like how much you expect your investments to grow each year (Expected Annual Return), how much prices might rise (Inflation Rate), and what percentage of your withdrawals will go to taxes (Retirement Tax Rate). All rates are entered as percentages.

The tool runs many possible "what if" scenarios for your retirement. Each scenario is a little different, because investment returns change from year to year. The Expected Annual Return is your best guess for how much your investments might grow on average, and the "Std Dev" (standard deviation) is a way to describe how much those returns might go up or down from year to year. The tool repeats this process thousands of times to show a range of possible outcomes. The chart shows the average result for each year, as well as the range where most outcomes fall (between the 5th and 95th percentiles), so you can see both typical and more extreme possibilities for your retirement plan.
